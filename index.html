Our Story

Founded in 2010, Cafe Amisha brings the authentic flavors of Italy to the heart of Bermondsey. Our passion for traditional Italian cuisine is matched only by our commitment to using the finest seasonal ingredients.

Every dish tells a story of Italian culinary heritage, from our handmade pasta to our wood-fired pizzas. Our chefs combine traditional techniques with modern creativity to deliver an unforgettable dining experience.

— The Amisha Family

Menu Highlights

Classical Carbonara

A timeless Roman specialty — al dente spaghetti tossed in a silky sauce of egg yolk and Pecorino Romano, studded with crispy guanciale and finished with freshly cracked black pepper.

Wood-Fired Pizza

Artisanal pizza baked in our traditional wood-fired oven — blistered crust, San Marzano tomatoes, buffalo mozzarella and fresh basil.

Risotto ai Frutti di Mare

Creamy Arborio rice simmered with fresh seafood, white wine and saffron for an authentic taste of the Italian coast.

Tiramisu

Espresso-soaked ladyfingers layered with velvety mascarpone cream and dusted with finest cocoa — a classic Italian finale.
