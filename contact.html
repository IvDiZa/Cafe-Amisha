Frequently Asked Questions

Quick answers to common questions about Cafe Amisha

Do you take reservations?
Yes, we highly recommend making a reservation, especially for weekend dining. You can book online through our website or call us directly at +44 (0)20 7237 8888.
Do you accommodate dietary restrictions?
Absolutely. We offer vegetarian, vegan, and gluten-free options. Please inform us of any dietary requirements when making your reservation, and our chefs will be happy to accommodate you.
Is there parking available?
While we don't have dedicated parking, there is paid street parking available in the area. We're also easily accessible via public transport, with London Bridge and Bermondsey stations within walking distance.
Do you offer private dining or event hosting?
Yes, we offer private dining options and can host events for groups. Please contact us directly to discuss your requirements and availability.
Is the restaurant wheelchair accessible?
Yes, our restaurant is fully wheelchair accessible, including our restrooms. If you have any specific accessibility needs, please let us know when making your reservation.
