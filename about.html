Our Story

A Bermondsey kitchen turned neighbourhood institution—seasonal Italian food from Amisha's family recipes.
By Cafe Amisha — updated 2025

Café Amisha is located on Grange Road SE1, very close to the sparkling and lively Bermondsey Street and within walking distance to Tower Bridge and London Bridge – famous touristic attractions in London.

From day one, the emphasis has been on quality and freshness and the restaurant uses only high quality fresh products sourced locally – fish and meat is delivered daily from local London markets and bread and cakes are baked in the restaurant.

Our Philosophy

All dishes are freshly prepared and cooked to order and served by friendly staff who will work hard to make your dining experience as enjoyable and memorable as possible. On a sunny day guests of Amisha can enjoy their food and drinks in the outside terrace (opened in April 2013).

With its simple and inspired cooking and exceptional customer service Amisha offers unique dining experience in Bermondsey area and with the highest quality fresh ingredients creates the genuine and authentic Italian 'Mamma style' food.

Meet Amisha

Founded from a love of traditional Italian cooking, Cafe Amisha started as a small neighbourhood kitchen focused on artisanal ingredients and authentic recipes. Over time it grew into a local destination where family recipes meet contemporary technique.

The team prioritises seasonal produce and trusted suppliers while blending time-honoured methods with modern presentation. Dishes are prepared from scratch — fresh pasta, slow-simmered sauces and house desserts are prepared daily to reflect the season.

Origins & Philosophy

Amisha's family roots inspired the menu: simple, ingredient-led cooking with an attention to detail. The kitchen sources local produce and select Italian imports — olive oils, tomatoes, cured items — which are used to build rich, balanced dishes.

Menus change with the seasons and often include a selection of small plates, handmade pasta and a rotating main course. The cafe also offers private dining and venue hire for larger gatherings.

The Team

Led by Amisha and a small devoted team of chefs and front-of-house staff, the cafe focuses on hospitality, consistency and culinary craft. The team collaborates with local producers to ensure the menu remains fresh and reflective of each season.
